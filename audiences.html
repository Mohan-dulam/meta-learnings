Meta Audience Targeting — Complete Guide

Audience selection tells Meta WHO should see your ad. The algorithm then learns WHICH users inside that audience convert.

3 Types of Audiences

1. Core Audience (Cold Traffic)

  • Location
  • Age
  • Gender
  • Interests
  • Behaviors
flowchart LR
BroadUsers --> InterestFilter --> EligibleAudience
Example: Gym owner runs ad targeting "Fitness & Supplements" interests. Meta finds users interested in workout content.
---

2. Custom Audience (Retargeting)

  • Website visitors
  • Instagram engagers
  • Video viewers
  • Lead form openers
flowchart TD
AdClick --> WebsiteVisit --> PixelCapture --> RetargetingAudience
---

3. Lookalike Audience (Scaling)

Meta analyzes converters and finds similar people.

  • 1% → high quality
  • 5% → scale
  • 10% → reach
Best source = Purchasers or Qualified Leads
