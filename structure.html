How Meta Ads Actually Work (Auction System)

Every time a user opens Instagram or Facebook, Meta runs an auction to decide which advertisement to show.

sequenceDiagram
User->>Meta App: Opens Feed
Meta App->>Meta Server: Request Ads
Meta Server->>Auction Engine: Evaluate Advertisers
Auction Engine->>Ranking System: Calculate Scores
Ranking System->>Winner Ad: Highest Value
Winner Ad->>User: Ad Displayed

Ad Rank Formula

Total Value = Bid + Estimated Action Rate + Ad Quality

  • Bid = how much advertiser willing to pay
  • Estimated Action Rate = chance of conversion
  • Ad Quality = engagement feedback
Example: Low budget but engaging ad can beat high budget poor ad.
